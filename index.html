💬 WhatsApp Location Import
How to save a WhatsApp location:
1️⃣ Open the location message in WhatsApp
2️⃣ Tap & hold the message → tap Copy
3️⃣ Paste below and tap Extract

Also works by copying the link from WhatsApp:
Tap the location → tap Open in Maps → copy the URL from browser → paste here
